MotionBalance: Motion Sickness Reduction

Reduce car sickness with real-time motion compensation. This app uses your iPhone's motion sensors to detect movement and adjusts your Mac's display to help reduce motion sickness.

Features:
• Real-time motion detection
• Adaptive visual compensation
• Bluetooth connectivity
• Customizable comfort settings
• Battery-optimized performance

Requirements:
• iPhone with motion sensors
• Mac with Bluetooth
• iOS 15.0 or later
• macOS 12.0 or later
